Discovering the joy of camping

And sublime quietude of solo camping

Camping can be a lot of fun and while going with a group, be-it friends or family, can be an enjoyable experience there is something to be said for finding a spot all to yourself to enjoy nature.

There are several resources highlighting camping (including solo camping) as well as for finding gear and locations to camp. Examples are:

  • Outdoor Magazine
  • The Dyrt: 5 Reasons to ditch your friends and try solo camping.
  • REI - The Joy of Solo Camping.
